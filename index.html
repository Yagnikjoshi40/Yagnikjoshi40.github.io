Our Story

12+ Years of
Dressing Dreams

SAKHI WOMEN'S WEAR is a proudly women-owned boutique in the heart of Borivali East — bringing runway-inspired Indian silhouettes, premium fabrics, and inclusive fits together under one welcoming roof.

For over 12 years, we have been the trusted styling destination for Mumbai's women — for weddings, festivals, Diwali celebrations, office days, and every beautiful moment in between.

We believe elegance is not a privilege — it is a birthright. And we built SAKHI to make sure every woman who walks through our doors walks out feeling exactly that.
