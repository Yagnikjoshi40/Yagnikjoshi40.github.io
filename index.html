Our Story

Born in Borivali,
Worn Everywhere

Sakhi Collection is a Borivali East boutique bringing runway-inspired Indian silhouettes, premium fabrics, and inclusive fits together under one roof. We started with one belief — that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary, not just on her wedding day, but every day.

From the lanes of Borivali to celebrations across Mumbai, Sakhi has become more than a store. It's a feeling of being seen, of walking out with something made for you.
